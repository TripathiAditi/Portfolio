About Advocate R.B. Choubey

"Client is my first priority. I understand that this may be an overwhelming, unfamiliar and stressful time for my clients. I strive to help them understand the intricacies of the legal system and keep them informed throughout the process."

— Adv. R.B. Choubey

Advocate Ram Badan Choubey is a Leading Advocate practising at the Jharkhand High Court, Ranchi. He is a former Additional Director (Human Resource), DVC under the Ministry of Power, Government of India.

With a demonstrated practical and ground experience of over 36 years, Advocate Choubey has exhibited a powerful combination of legal expertise and excellent communication skills, including hands-on litigation experience across diverse legal domains.

His specialisations include labour law, service law, tariff cases, constitutional law, matters relating to industrial disputes, arbitration, and negotiations on criminal matters relating to labour laws.
